Our Story

We build software Indian businesses actually need

Founded in Tamil Nadu, AMTechnexus has spent 5 years building software purpose-built for Indian industries — not generic tools that businesses have to adapt to.

Who We Are

Technology partner to 500+ businesses

AMTechnexus Private Limited is an Indian software company based in Tamil Nadu. We build four products — NexusPOS, NexusAI, NexusBilling, and NexusApartments — used by restaurants, garages, and apartment communities across India.

Our belief is simple: Indian businesses deserve software built specifically for them — with GST compliance, UPI payments, and workflows that match how business is actually done here.
